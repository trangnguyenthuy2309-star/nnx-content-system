# Style Preset — Editorial 3D

> Photoreal 3D objects on cream pedestals, magazine-editorial aesthetic.
> **Best for:** storytelling, history, case studies, narrative content.

---

```
=== STYLE PRESET: EDITORIAL 3D ===

Aesthetic reference: high-end finance magazine editorial photography +
3D product photography. Think Monocle magazine cover crossed with WSJ
Saturday section infographic. Premium, narrative, slightly cinematic.

OBJECT TREATMENT:
- Photoreal 3D objects (coins, stacked banknotes, ancient ledgers,
  small bank buildings, vintage scales, abacuses, smartphones with
  finance apps, credit cards, sealed envelopes) rendered with
  realistic materials, warm studio lighting, soft shadows.
- Each object sits on a small cylindrical CREAM pedestal (#FAF6F0 with
  subtle marble or stone texture). Pedestals vary in height to create
  rhythm in compositions.
- Objects feel slightly oversized, hero-like — but never cartoonish.
  Material details (metal patina, paper grain, fabric weave) visible.

LIGHTING:
- Single soft warm key light from upper-left, gentle fill from right.
- Soft contact shadows under each pedestal — no harsh hard shadows.
- Subtle rim light on objects sitting against darker areas.
- Overall warmth: late-afternoon-studio feel.

TEXTURE & GRAIN:
- Very light film grain across full image (~5%) for editorial feel.
- Pedestals: matte cream stone with subtle veining.
- Background: solid cream #FAF6F0 with very faint world-map outline
  or dotted grid at 5–8% opacity. Never busy.

DECORATION MOTIFS:
- Vintage globe outline (single thin line, jade-tinted) at top-right,
  ~6% opacity.
- Tiny architecture silhouette (Greek temple / classical bank facade)
  at bottom-right corner, very faint.
- Small geometric flourishes near titles: thin underline strokes,
  single sparkle/star accent next to display titles.
- Dotted lines connecting timeline items when slide shows progression.
- Tiny secondary doodles (faint coin, faint chart) at edges as
  marginalia — extremely subtle.

TYPOGRAPHY (visual feel only — no specific font name):
- Display title: heavy serif OR semi-condensed bold sans, 2 lines,
  with the second line in terracotta #C9572C. Slight character
  spacing tightening for impact.
- Subtitle: medium-weight, with vertical terracotta bar to the left.
- Body: clean modern sans-serif, dark charcoal #2D2A26, 1.5 line-height.
- Tag pill text: small ALL-CAPS, jade or charcoal background.

CALLOUT BOXES:
- Rounded rectangles, cream fill with thin jade #2A8A7F border (1.5px),
  small lightbulb / chart / shield icon on the left in jade,
  one-line key insight on the right with one word highlighted in
  terracotta.

OVERALL VIBE: editorial, premium, narrative — feels like a Vietnamese
business magazine spread that takes finance seriously without being cold.

=== END STYLE PRESET ===
```
