```
=== NNX INSURANCE BRAND DNA (do not modify) ===

Brand: NNX INSURANCE — Vietnamese broker insurance company for non-life insurance.
Audience: Vietnamese insurance brokers, age 25–55, urban, financially curious.
Voice: warm, expert, grounded, never alarmist, never salesy.

COLOR PALETTE (use exactly these hex values, no substitutes):
- Green #0DB04B — primary text, CTA, dark backgrounds
- Yellow #FFCB05 — primary accent, key numbers, highlight, badge, second-line titles
- Dark Green #276031 — secondary accent, tags, icons, sub-borders
- Orange #F794D — small highlights, badges only (≤5% of canvas)
- Cream #FAF6F0 — primary background, soft surfaces

COLOR USAGE RATIO PER SLIDE:
- Cream background: ~65% of canvas
- Green (text + dark zones): ~15%
- Yellow (accent, CTA, key words): ~10%
- Dark green (sub accent, tags, icons): ~5%
- Orange (tiny highlight only): ≤5%

NEVER use:
- Cold corporate blue
- Neon colors (electric blue, hot pink, lime green)
- Rainbow / multi-hue gradients
- Pure white #FFFFFF backgrounds (use cream #FAF6F0)
- Pure black #000000 (use charcoal #2D2A26)

TYPOGRAPHY FEEL:
- Display title: bold, confident, slightly tight letter-spacing.
  Often 2 lines — first line in charcoal, second line in terracotta.
- Body text: clean modern sans-serif, easy reading, line-height 1.4–1.6.
- All visible text in Vietnamese with full diacritics.
- Avoid full ALL-CAPS for long phrases (allowed only for short tags / 1–2 words).
- No drop shadows on text. No neon glow.

MOOD: warm, earthy, editorial, professional. Inspired by quality finance
magazines (Monocle, Bloomberg Businessweek) but with Vietnamese cultural
warmth — not cold Wall Street aesthetics.

LAYOUT ANCHORS (must appear on every slide unless layout explicitly omits):
- Top-left: tag pill (jade or charcoal background) with small icon and
  short label like "FINANCE SERIES" or "GIAI ĐOẠN 1" or "BƯỚC 02".
- Bottom-right: page counter circle "X/N" (e.g., "1/6"). Cream fill,
  charcoal text, thin charcoal border.
- Subtle background decoration: faint world map / dotted grid /
  geometric outline at <8% opacity — never compete with content.

LANGUAGE — VIETNAMESE FIRST:
- All visible text must be in Vietnamese with correct diacritics.
- Verify each word: "và" not "vả", "tài chính" not "tỉ chính",
  "ngân hàng" not "ngân ngân hàng", "quản lý" not "quẫn lý".
- No English placeholder text. No Latin filler ("Lorem ipsum").
- No stray Chinese / Japanese / Korean characters as decoration
  (unless content topic specifically requires them).

=== END BRAND DNA ===
```
